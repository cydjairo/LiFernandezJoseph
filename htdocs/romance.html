School/Campus Romance:

"This is a coming-of-age series about the friendship of college athletes reaching their dreams while falling in love and getting into romantic relationships along the way. Who knew that a simple accident between Jung Joon-Hyung, a male college swimmer, and Kim Bok-Joo, a female college weightlifter, would spark a connection with just a missing handkerchief."

Some platforms to watch on:
KissAsian, Viu, RakutenViki, DramaCool, Netflix, iQIYI

"This is a story about an average highschool girl, Geum Jan-Di, whose life gets caught up with the richest and most spoiled boys in her school, the F4, who was lead by Koo Jun-Pyo. It was based on the Japanese shōjo manga series Boys Over Flowers (花より男子, Hana Yori Dango) written by Yoko Kamio."


Some platforms to watch on:
KissAsian, Viu, RakutenViki, DramaCool, Netflix, iQIYI
